Survival Of The Cyborgs
Brief
"Round 1 – BattleshipSink all of the opponent’s ships by correctly guessing their location. Players will lie down in the grid drawn on the floor of the room and then the team’s grids will be separated by a barricade in between. Players will be given balls that they have to aim at the enemy team and 1st team to loose all 3 ships gets eliminated.
Round 2 – Foreman’s Treasure The parts of an assembly will be scattered throughout the campus/ block. Crack the technical clues given to them (related to senses and sensors) and do the given task Successfully. The list of parts and procedure is the last clue that will be give and the team to assemble and submit the assembly in the quickest time/1st wins"
Participation: Team of 3

Registration Fee: 100 INR per team
Rules:
Round 1
1. All the ships are on the board, they are hit by calling out the coordinates.
2. Coordinates should be guessed as the players can’t see each others boards.
3. Teams of only 3. Each team is allowed 5 ships.
4. Teams take turns in guessing the coordinates .If the coordinates are right, the team should tell ‘Hit and Sunk’
5. The game ends when all the ships of one team are sunk.
Round 2
1. In the second round, assembly of a machine part is given, eg: tailstock.
2. The list of parts and procedure is first given to the dumb person, who tells it out using actions. The deaf has to figure out what his actions, and help the blind assemble the parts.
3. Only the blind person is allowed to touch the parts.
4. Team which does it the quickest wins the event.:

Brief

Registration Fee: 200 INR per team
Rules:
Round 1: Comprises of a quiz with multiple choice questions. There will be a total of 20 questions, which have to be answered in 20 minutes. Top teams qualify for the next round.

Round 3: In this round, the teams will be given an incorrect circuit diagram. With the help of the hints and wifi provided, they will have to find out the correct circuit and implement the same in the lab. This event has a time limit of 20 minutes. The fastest team to finish wins the prize.
